SURREALISM

STOLEN PARA"DIES"

In the illustration, we can see a picture of an eye and buildings within and our nature that is slowly dying. This drawing shows one of the common and vital problem of this world which is pollution. As years pass by, pollution got worse due to industrialization which resulted to global warming. The extreme heat of the sun that touches us, the massive wild fire in some parts of the world and the death of wild animals are the visible evidences of this problem. The drawing depicts that we, human beings are blind when it comes to our wrong doings. We still keep on doing things that slowly kill our mother earth, not realizing that we are slowly killing the place that we depend our life on.
